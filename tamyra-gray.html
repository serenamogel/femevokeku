A Glance At Tamyra Gray - Bio, Fact, Net Worth and Husband

Tamyra Gray is a gorgeous and talented American actress, singer, and songwriter. She is well-known among the public for her role as Aisha Clemens in the TV series Boston Public.

She was born on July 26, 1979, in Takoma Park, Maryland, the United States of America, and holds Leo as her birth sign.

Early Life

The Tru Calling actress Tamyra was born and raised in Maryland, the United States by her parents but she has not revealed any sort of information regarding her parents on any of her social media.

She was passionate about singing and acting from an early age and she used to participate in many singing competitions during her childhood.

Additionally, she was also interested in beauty and fashion. In the year 1998, she won the title of ''Miss Atlanta''.

Where Did Tamyra Graduate From?

Tamyra is a well-educated actress who finished her early education at a private school in her hometown. After that, she completed her high school education at Stone Mountain School which is situated in Stone Mountain, Georgia.

Moreover, she then enrolled in Georgia State University to pursue her higher education in her aspired subject. The University is located in Atlanta, Georgia, from where she graduated in business law and management. 

Husband of Tamyra Gray

Tamyra is a married woman and she is currently living in a married relationship with Sam Watters who is a music producer as well as a songwriter by profession. The romantic couple tied their knot on September 2, 2006, in a private ceremony that was held in Capri, Italy. 

They exchanged their vows in the presence of close friends and family where they invited only 45 personal guests. They captured many beautiful photos of their wedding and shared them in public to update their fans about their wedding.

Additionally, the couple welcomed their first child on March 23 and named their daughter Sienna Marie. According to Married Biography, Tamyra revealed the news about her baby through American Entertainment Weekly.
