Gideon Is Deaf With His Right Ear

Yes, it is true that Gideon Glick is deaf in his right ear. The condition is called Unilateral Hearing loss, and he became deaf in one ear at the beginning of his birth. This condition has also led to him facing some problems.

Glick has to struggle a bit, especially while singing on stage. He has talked about how an inability to hear from one ear has led him to struggle to understand the harmony of what he is listening to. This has a direct effect on his performance. But it hasn't stopped him from continuing to do what he loves.
