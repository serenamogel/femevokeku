Tonights WWE Raw Preview; Slammys, 3 Hour Show, Matches

Tonight, WWE is broadcasting this week's edition of WWE Raw from the Scope Arena in Norfolk, VA. The show is a three hour special, which airs at 8pm EST, and will feature the 2011 Slammy Awards.

Additionally, it was announced last week that the show will be headlined by John Cena vs Mark Henry in a non-title match.

The advertised dark match main event is CM Punk (c) vs Alberto Del Rio vs The Miz in a Triple Threat WWE Title Match.

The following Smackdown talents are being advertised for the show: Randy Orton, Big Show, Sheamus, Mark Henry, Cody Rhodes, Wade Barrett, Ted DiBiase, and Daniel Bryan.
