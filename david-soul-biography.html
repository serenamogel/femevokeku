Who is David Soul?

David is best known for the role of Officer Davis in Magnum Force (Action Drama) in 1973, Det. Ken ‘Hutch’ Hutchinson in Starsky and Hutch (TV Series) in 1975, and Original Hutch in Starsky & Hutch (Comedy Crime) in 2004.
