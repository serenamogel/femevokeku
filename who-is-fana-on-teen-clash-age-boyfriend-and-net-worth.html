Fana Teen Clash: Age Explored

Fana from Teen Clash is a twenty-two-year artist. She was born Fatima Louise Lagueras on April 23, 2000.

The name Fana was coined as her stage name. She sang “Runaway Baby” in her audition for the first season of the Voice Teens Philippines. 

The event was in 2017 when Fana was only seventeen years. Sadly, she got eliminated in the Knockout Round.

Later, at nineteen, the young artist joined the ABS-CBN singing competition Idol Philippines. She ended up as one of the top eight finalists in the show, where Vice Ganda, James Reid, Regine Velasquez, and Moira Dela Torre were judges.

She became the performer of the said show after appearing in the ABS-CBN noontime show ASAP in July of the same year.
