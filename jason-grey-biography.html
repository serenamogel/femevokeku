Jason Grey Bio (Wiki)

Who is Jason Grey?

Jason Grey is an American contemporary Christian singer and songwriter, and he has released several albums independently under his real name, Jason Gay.

He was signed to Centricity Music to release more albums with them under the name Jason Grey in 2006.

Jason Grey: Professional Life and Career

He came up with four independent records before signing with Centricity Music in 2006. He brought his first major label record ‘All the Lovely Losers’ in 2007. ChristianityToday.com named Jason as ‘Chritian music’s best-kept secrets’.

He collaborated with many artists like Michael W. Smith, Steven Curtis Chapman, Sanctus Real(music band), and many more. ‘Nothing Is Wasted’ which was his No.1 single, reached the top list on the Soft AC Chart in May 2013 and remained there for almost nine weeks.

His song ‘Love Will Have the Final Word’ was released on March 4, 2014, in which the song speaks about the redeeming power of unfailing love, closure, and decisiveness.

His single ‘With Every Act of Love’ helped him to become the most successful lunch on the radio in Centricity Music which reached Billboard’s Top Ten AC Indicator chart and was also selected for Top 20 on the National Audience Chart and became successful to come in No. 1 radio hit.

Jason Grey joined The Bible: Son of God 2014 Tour on March 20, 2014, with artists such as Natalie Grant, Francesca Battistelli, Sidewalk Prophets, Chris August, and Meredith Andrews.

He released ‘Love Will Have the Final Word: PostScript’ on June 9, 2015, which features remixes, over-cuts, and demos from the previous album ‘love Will Have the Final Word’, ‘Glow In the Dark’ and ‘One Voice’. His studio album ‘Where the Light Gets In’ was released in collaboration with Centricity Music on June 17, 2016.

Jason Grey: Lifetime Achievement and Awards

He received two ASCAP Performance Awards for his songs ‘More Like Falling In Love’ in 2011 and ‘Good to Be Alive’ in 2012. His single ‘Nothing Is Wasted’ released in 2012 was named Album of the Year by critics and became Top 5 radio singles. He was honored with the ‘Most Performed Song Award’ for the song ‘Good to Be Alive’ at ASCAP’s 35th Annual Christian Music Awards.
