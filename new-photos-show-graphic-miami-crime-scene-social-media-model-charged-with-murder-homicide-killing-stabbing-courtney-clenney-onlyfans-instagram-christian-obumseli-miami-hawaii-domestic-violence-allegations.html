New photos show graphic Miami crime scene, social media model charged with murder

Courtney Clenney, 26, is accused of killing Christian Obumseli on April 3.

**WARNING: This story contains graphic content. Viewer discretion is advised.**

One photo shows Clenney facing the camera in a black robe, tank top and bloody NBA sweatpants. Her eyelashes are in tatters.

Another photo is a tight shot of her hands. Her fingernails are bloody. An orange, fake fingernail stands out on her left index finger. Crime scene detectives even took photos of her bloody feet, among other areas.

The other photos show pools of blood throughout the Miami high-rise condo, with boxes and other personal items scattered about. A shoe sits on its side next to a pile of debris. One photo shows a trail of blood from Unit 2201 to the elevator on the 22nd floor.

Authorities took photos of the bloodied body of Obumseli as he lay dead on a gurney, and of a bloody knife.

There's also a letter that investigators took pictures of. It's ripped up, but investigators made an effort to piece it together. It reads in part, "You are hone(s)tly the sweetest and most annoying woman I know (happy face) but I would never trade you for anything."

Police have said in the past the couple had a rocky relationship. In February, the couple had a fight in the condo elevator. It was all caught on camera.

But Clenney's attorneys said she's the victim, adding that she stabbed her boyfriend in self-defense. They pointed to another moment caught on camera, during which she told police on April 2, "I'm scared of him at this point."

One day later, Obumseli was found dead from stab wounds. U.S. Marshals arrested Clenney in Hawaii in August.
