Sam Claflin Weight Loss Journey; Before &amp; After Photos How Much LBS Did He Lose?

Actor Sam Claflin amazed fans with his weight loss journey. Reportedly, he lost over 40 pounds for the 2016 Romantic film, Me Before You.

Sam Claflin is a renowned English actor, popularly known for the 2011 film titled Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides as Philip Swift. He gained the attention of the public for The Hunger Games film series (2013).

In 2010, Claflin debuted his acting career with two television miniseries namely The Pillars of the Earth, and Any Human Heart. He also portrayed the role of footballer Duncan Edwards in the BBC TV drama United.

Currently, Actor Sam Claflin is cast as Henry Copper in the film “Book Of Love” (2022) directed by Analeine Cal y Mayor. He is starring alongside Veronia Echegui, Fernando Becerril, Horacio Garcia Rojas, and Lucy Punch.

Sam Claflin Weight Loss Journey Explored

In the romantic drama Me Before You (2016), Sam Claflin was cast as a wealthy young man, Will Traynor. As per sources, he lost a significant amount of his weight for his role in the film.

Claflin had to get in peak physical condition to portrayed the role of Will Traynor in My Before You. He transformed his body with at least one long and hard daily session in the gym for the shirtless scene in the film.

Sam Claflin followed a very strict diet with little sleep as he mentioned in his Instagram caption. Within three months, he shed over 3 stones with hard training and diet.

The romantic drama, Me Before You was released on 3 June 2016, directed by Thea Sharrock, and succeeded to collect $208.3 million in the box office. We must say the film is Will’s journey as he worked so hard for his role.
