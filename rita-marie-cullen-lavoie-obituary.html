Obituary: Rita Marie (Cullen) LaVoie

It is with great sadness that we announce the passing of Rita Marie (Cullen) LaVoie, a beloved resident of Grand Rapids, Michigan. On December 27, 2023, Rita Marie left this world, leaving behind a void that will be deeply felt by her family and friends. We gather here today to honor her memory and celebrate the remarkable life she lived.

Passing and Condolences

As we come to terms with the loss of Rita Marie, we invite you to join us in expressing your condolences and sharing your memories on the memorial page dedicated to her. This will serve as a space for us to come together as a community and offer support to her grieving family during this difficult time. Your kind words and heartfelt messages will provide comfort and solace as they navigate through their grief.

Predeceased Family Members

Rita Marie was preceded in death by her beloved husband, George LaVoie Sr., her son George LaVoie II, and her brothers Donald Cullen and James Cullen. Their passing has left a profound impact on the family, and their memories will forever be cherished. We remember them today and honor the legacy they have left behind.

Surviving Family Members

Rita Marie’s legacy lives on through her children: Julianne Jaksa (John), Denise Dame (Blair), John LaVoie (Martha), Thomas LaVoie, Daniel LaVoie (Nancy), Sarah LaVoie, Stephen LaVoie (Donna), Mary LaVoie (Ray), and Joseph LaVoie. They carry with them the values and love instilled by their mother, ensuring that her spirit will continue to shine brightly. Additionally, her sisters-in-law, Jean Cullen and Elizabeth Sharpy, stand alongside the family, providing unwavering support and strength.

Furthermore, Rita Marie’s impact extends to her 19 grandchildren and 13 great-grandchildren, who will carry forward her legacy with pride. Through their actions and achievements, they will honor the memory of their beloved grandmother and great-grandmother.

Let us come together as a community to celebrate the life of Rita Marie (Cullen) LaVoie, a remarkable woman who touched the hearts of many. May her soul rest in eternal peace.

Funeral Details

As we gather to bid farewell to Rita Marie (Cullen) LaVoie, we would like to provide you with the necessary information regarding the funeral arrangements. It is important for us to come together as a community to honor her life and pay our final respects.

A Christian burial mass will be held to commemorate the life of Rita Marie. This sacred ceremony will serve as a time of reflection, prayer, and remembrance. It is an opportunity for us to come together and find solace in our shared faith as we celebrate the life of a remarkable individual.

Location and Time

The Christian burial mass for Rita Marie (Cullen) LaVoie will take place at the St. Paul Church, located at 2750 Burton Street Southeast in Grand Rapids, Michigan. This beautiful and serene setting will provide a fitting backdrop for us to honor her memory.
