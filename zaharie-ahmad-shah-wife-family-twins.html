Zaharie had been a pilot with Malaysia Airlines for over 30 years and had logged more than 18,000 flight hours. He was highly respected in the aviation industry and had a reputation for being an experienced and skilled pilot.

After the disappearance of Flight MH370, Zaharie’s personal and professional background came under intense scrutiny as investigators tried to determine what had happened to the aircraft.
