Charmeleon (Japanese: リザード Lizardo) is a Fire-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It is part of the Hidden Fates expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Hidden Fates expansion, first released in the Charizard Deck of the Sun & Moon Family Pokémon Card Game in Japan. The Battle Academy, the English equivalent of the Family Pokémon Card Game also included this card in its Charizard Deck. Each card in the deck has the silhouette of Charizard and a number in the bottom right of the card. Three copies of Charmeleon numbered 15, 30, and 51 are included in the deck.

Trivia

Origin

Slash and Flamethrower are both moves in the Pokémon games that Charmeleon can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon X.
