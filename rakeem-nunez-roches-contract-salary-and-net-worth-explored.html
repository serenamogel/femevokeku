Rakeem Nunez-Roches contract, salary and net worth explored

Rakeem Nathan Nuez-Roches, a nose tackle for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers of the National Football League, was born in Belize on July 3, 1993. At the 2015 NFL Draft, he was selected by the Kansas City Chiefs in the sixth round (217th overall).

When he was eight years old, Nuez-Roches moved to Alabama from Belize with his family He began playing football as a student at Phenix City, Alabama’s Central High School. He made 132 tackles, 17 sacks, and four forced fumbles in high school football.
