Career

It was announced on 17 June 2011 that N-Dubz had left their American record deal in the midst of rumors that the band had split up. His first taste of solo stardom came as a guest artist on Tinchy Stryder’s single “Spaceship,” released in June 2011. Dappy’s single “Beautiful Me” was released on 29 March 2015 and reached number 19 in the United Kingdom. In 2016, Dappy released the singles “Hip Hip Hooray” and “Kiss” and the freestyle “Messi” these songs were released again through an independent label/management partnership called LEX Music Corp.

On 31 July 2017, Dappy released the single Straight Facts” Again, this was part of a new management agreement with PlayDirty manager Vash, who currently manages Krept and Konan. The music video was released on the same day. On 12 October 2017, Dappy released his second single under the PlayDirty management titled “Spotlight” on 56 in the UK Singles Chart.

In 2018, he released his single “Oh My” featuring British rapper Ay Em. It was 23 on the charts. He followed this up with the song “All We Know” which peaked at 64 and released an acoustic on YouTube called “Count on Me.”In 2019, Dappy collaborated with Tory Lanez releasing ‘Not Today[11]’.
