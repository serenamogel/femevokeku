Oscar Goodman Biography

Oscar Goodman (Full Name: Oscar Baylin Goodman) who was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, U.S. is an American attorney and politician. From 1999 to 2011, Goodman was the mayor of Las Vegas, Nevada. His wife, Carolyn Goodman, succeeded him as mayor in 2011. Goodman is an Independent.

Goodman and his wife’s Carolyn were active in the local Jewish federation soon after they moved to Las Vegas in 1964 as well, with Carolyn eventually serving as head of the federation’s women’s divisions.

Also, he represented former San Diego Mayor Roger Hedgecock, who was convicted of accepting illegal campaign contributions and eventually forced to resign. Hedgecock was later cleared of all charges on appeal. He served as president of the National Association of Criminal Defense Lawyers from 1980 to 1981.

Robert Rose who is an ethics watchdog filed a complaint with the Nevada Commission on Ethics, asking the commission to clarify Goodman’s affiliation with his son Ross’s law firm 16th September 2004.

The mayor explained his name on the letterhead is a way of informing out of state law firms that Ross Goodman is his son in a statement.
