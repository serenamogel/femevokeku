8 Things You Didnt Know About Rahul Dev

➡ Rahul Dev Biography

Rahul Dev is another well-known actor who is known for his best-loved acting skills, and also a great model. He predominantly works in the Hindi, and South Indian film industry where he acted in Bollywood, Tamil, Malayalam, Telugu, and Kannada as well. Apart from acting and modeling, he appeared on the reality television show named ‘Bigg Boss’ season 10. In 2000, the actor debuted through a film named ‘Champion’ in which he played a villainous role for which he got nominated for the Filmfare Best Villain Award in 2001. In 2011, the actor moved to the Punjabi film industry and appeared in a debut film named ‘Dharti’ in which he appeared as Villain. Later, he then made appearances in many Hindi language films. Well, to know more about his personal and professional life, let us have a look at these things we are sure you haven’t heard yet!

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Rahul Dev

1- He & Mukul Dev

Rahul Dev is an elder brother of Mukul Dev who is also an actor and previously worked in many Bollywood movies. Mukul and Rahul Dev are sons of Mr. Hari Dev who served as a Police Officer.

2- Mostly Played Roles

Rahul Dev is one of the most popular actors who has played a variety of roles and the mostly played roles are villainous for which he got much acclaim and it became his identity that he always appeared as an antagonist.

3- Worked in Multilingual Movies

Rahul Dev has worked in many Hindi language films including Hindi, Punjabi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, and Malayalam as well. This way, he has built an extensive, broad career in the field of acting. This is an amazing victory.

4- Worked on Small Screen

The actor has also worked on the small screen and appeared in a television debut serial named ‘Devon Ka Dev… Mahadev’ in which he portrayed the role of the demon Arunasur. It was a mythological television series that was aired in 2013.

5- Comes from an educated family

Rahul Dev comes from an educated family as his father served as a Police Officer who died in April 2019. While his mother, Anoop Kaushal is also working as a teacher, and his younger brother, Mukul Dev is also an actor.

6- Loves Gymming

The actor, Rahul Dev is a fitness lover and never skips gymming and his workout regimen. Also, he follows a proper nutrient diet to keep himself fit and healthy throughout the day.

7- Favorite Actor

Here’s a chance to know about your favorite celebrity’s most favorite actor, and we are revealing that Rahul Dev’s most favorite Hollywood actor is Arnold Schwarzenegger who is famous for his extraordinary acting chops.

8- His Belief

After playing negative roles, Rahul Dev has a core belief that he is made for playing negative roles as he is the capable man for portraying villainous roles in many Indian films and television series as well. Woaaah.. that’s confidence!
