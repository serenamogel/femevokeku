12 Most Gorgeous Blue-Eyed Women in Hollywood

“The Eyes, Chico, They Never Lie,” whoever coined this phrase must be referring to the fact that eyes are the most striking and expressive feature on a person’s face. The eyes can’t hide a person’s feelings or emotions, which are otherwise entirely concealed by other facial features.

Different colors, shapes, and sizes of eyes make them look unique, and a perfect combination of all three makes eyes look beautiful. The Iris of an eye is responsible for different colors. Lack of concentration of melanin in the stroma of the Iris is responsible for blue eyes.

Blue eye color is gradually receding in human beings and is mostly found in parts of Europe, Northern areas, and Ireland. People with blue eyes are becoming extinct in America, so anyone with blue eyes can easily steal the show anywhere.
