Lindsay Lohan’s Early Life

Lindsay Lohan was born on July 2, 1986, in New York City. She grew up on Long Island, New York. Her father is a former Wall Street trader who has been in trouble with the law on several occasions. Her mother is a former singer and dancer. Lohan has three younger siblings: Michael Jr., Ali, and Cody.

Lohan was homeschooled from the age of 11 onward. Her parents, Dina and Michael Lohan, divorced in 2007.

Lindsay Lohan’s Career Highlights

Lindsay Lohan began her career as a child model, signing with Ford Models at the age of three. She appeared in numerous TV commercials and landed her first television role in 1996 on “Another World.”

Director Nancy Meyers cast Lohan in the 1998 remake of “The Parent Trap,” which earned her a Young Artist Award. She signed a three-movie deal with Disney and went on to star in “Life Size” and “Get a Clue.”

Lohan’s breakout role came in 2003’s “Freaky Friday” opposite Jamie Lee Curtis, for which she won the 2004 MTV Movie Award for Breakthrough Performance. She starred in “Confessions of a Teenage Drama Queen” and “Mean Girls,” winning four Teen Choice Awards for the latter. Lohan also appeared in “Herbie: Fully Loaded,” “Just My Luck,” and “A Prairie Home Companion.”

Aside from her acting career, Lohan has also modeled for various fashion brands and released her own line of leggings and self-tanning spray.

Lohan’s Music Career

Lohan pursued a music career while also acting, contributing tracks to her movies “Freaky Friday” and “Confessions of a Teenage Drama Queen.” Her debut album, “Speak,” was released in December 2004 and reached number four on the music charts. Her second album, “A Little More Personal (Raw),” was released in December 2005 and peaked at number 20 on the charts.

Lindsay Lohan’s Personal Life

Lohan has been romantically linked to several high-profile figures, including actor Wilmer Valderrama, Hard Rock Cafe heir Harry Morton, and DJ Samantha Ronson. In 2016, she was engaged to London-based Russian millionaire Egor Tarabasov, but the couple reportedly split in mid-2017 amidst accusations of abuse and theft.

Lohan has struggled with substance abuse and has been arrested multiple times for DUIs. Her personal problems have resulted in the loss of film roles and intense negative media attention.

Lindsay Lohan’s Financial Struggles

Lindsay Lohan earned over $27 million from movie salaries, but faced legal and personal problems that drained her net worth. She posed nude for Playboy in 2011 for $1 million, when she was reportedly close to bankruptcy. In 2012, the IRS seized her bank accounts to pay off her $233,000 in unpaid back taxes. In 2013, she earned $2 million for interviews with Oprah Winfrey, but a large portion went towards taxes, rehab fees, and debts.
